Ghormeh Sabzi (Lamb Stew)

By: Kamrun Samadnejad

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. boneless lamb
  • 1 whole onion; finely chopped
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 tspn. turmeric
  • 6 dried lemons
  • 3/4 cup kidney beans
  • salt & pepper; to taste
  • 1 cup green onion; finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup parsley; finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro; finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup dried fenugreek leaves; finely chopped

Preparation

  1. cut meat into small cubes
  2. fry onion over medium heat until golden
  3. add turmeric and fry for 2 more minutes
  4. increase heat, add meat cubes and stir over high heat until meat begins to turn brown
  5. reduce heat
  6. add water, drained kidney beans, salt & pepper (to taste)
  7. cover and simmer for about an hour or until meat is tender
  8. add prepared vegetables to frying pan and fry over medium heat until wilted
  9. add vegetables and dried lemons to the meat and kidney beans and simmer for another 15 minutes
  10. taste test, then serve over rice
